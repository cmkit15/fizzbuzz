Fizz Buzz

Fizz Buzz is a program that prints the numbers from 1 to 100.
Fizz will represent the multiples of the first number.
Buzz will represent the multiples of the second number.
Fizzbuzz will represent the multiples of both numbers.

Enter two numbers between 1 & 100
